This is a verification bot that verifies your reddit account with
your discord account. Here are all the commands:

`~logout` - logs you out of the account you have linked.
`~login <username>` - logs you into your reddit account.
`~whois <@mention> | <nickname>`  - gives the reddit of the person you mention.
`~find <username>` - finds the discord account of who the reddit account belongs to.
